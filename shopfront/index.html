Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

Why are you so much cheaper than an agency?

Because we're two people, not an office full of them. No account managers, no rent, and we build on hosting that's free. You pay for the work, not the overhead.

Do I need to write the words or take photos?

No. Tell us about your business and we'll draft all the words for you to approve. Photos from your phone are honestly fine — we'll make them look good.

What's a "web address" and do I need one?

It's the name people type in, like yourshop.com. It costs about $15 a year, it's registered in your name, and yes — you want one. We'll help you pick it and set it up.

Are there monthly fees?

No. The price on this page is the whole price. The only recurring cost is the web address (about $15 a year), and that goes to the registrar, not to us.

How fast is "about a week", really?

For a one-pager: your free mockup usually within a few days, then about a week from the moment you approve it. The Full Shop takes about two. If something will take longer, we'll say so up front.

What if I need changes later?

Send them over — small changes are $30 a time, done within a couple of days. Or we hand you the keys and show you how to make simple updates yourself, free.

What happens if you two disappear?

You'd be fine. You own the files, the words and the web address — nothing is locked to us. Any developer could pick it up tomorrow. That's the point of "you own everything".
